# Local Law 97 (LL97) — New York City

## Overview
Local Law 97 is New York City's landmark climate legislation, part of the Climate Mobilization Act (2019). It sets greenhouse gas emissions limits for buildings over 25,000 square feet, covering approximately 50,000 buildings that account for nearly 60% of NYC's building emissions.

## Compliance Timeline
- **2024–2029**: First compliance period. Buildings must meet initial carbon caps based on occupancy group.
- **2030–2034**: Second compliance period. Stricter limits reduce allowable emissions by approximately 40% from 2005 levels.
- **2035–2039**: Third compliance period. Further tightening toward 80% reduction.
- **2050**: Net-zero target for all covered buildings.

## Emissions Limits (tCO2e per sq ft per year)
| Building Type | 2024 Limit | 2030 Limit |
|---------------|-----------|-----------|
| Office (Group B) | 0.00846 | 0.00453 |
| Residential (Group R) | 0.00675 | 0.00407 |
| Retail (Group M) | 0.01181 | 0.00426 |
| Hotel (Group R-1) | 0.00987 | 0.00526 |
| Healthcare (Group I) | 0.02381 | 0.01207 |

## Penalties
- **$268 per metric ton of CO2** over the limit per year.
- For a 500,000 sq ft office building exceeding by 20%, this could be **$500,000–$1,000,000 annually**.
- No penalty cap — exposure increases linearly with overage.
- Good-faith compliance efforts may reduce penalties (DOB discretion).

## Mitigation Strategies
1. **Electrification**: Replace steam/gas heating with air-source or ground-source heat pumps. Eliminates on-site combustion emissions.
2. **Building envelope upgrades**: Insulation, window replacement, air sealing to reduce heating/cooling loads.
3. **LED lighting and controls**: Reduces electricity consumption by 40–60%.
4. **Building Management System (BMS)**: Smart controls for HVAC scheduling, demand response, and optimization.
5. **Renewable Energy Credits (RECs)**: Purchase RECs from clean energy sources to offset grid emissions.
6. **On-site solar PV**: Reduces grid electricity consumption and associated emissions.
7. **Prescriptive pathway**: Follow specific DOB-approved measures for compliance without emissions tracking.

## NYC Accelerator Program
Free technical advisory service for NYC buildings pursuing LL97 compliance:
- Free energy audits and decarbonization roadmaps
- Project management support for retrofits
- Help navigating available incentives and financing options
