The Problem

Climate risk data is
fragmented & manual

Brokers and asset managers spend days stitching together flood maps, weather data, building codes, and regulatory requirements — for a single property.

Data sources to check
8–12 per property
Time per risk assessment
3–5 days manual
Strategy creation effort
Weeks per client

Two disconnected worlds

Risk scoring — data science team runs models, produces reports that sit in spreadsheets


Strategy creation — sustainability consultants manually build decks, often without access to the latest risk data


The risk score never flows into the strategy. The strategy never references the risk.

The Solution

One pipeline.
Risk → Intelligence → Action.

ClimateNexus chains climate-risk scoring directly into an AI sustainability strategist — so the risk score drives the recommendations.

Stage 1

Multi-Hazard
Climate Risk Scoring

ML-powered engine that ingests open climate data and produces an explainable composite risk score (0–100) per property.

Hazard Dimensions

🌊

Flood

FEMA zones, elevation, historical claims

🌡️

Heat Stress

NOAA projections, cooling degree days

🔥

Wildfire

NIFC perimeters, burn probability

⚖️

Transition Risk

LL97, BERDO, building performance standards — regulatory exposure

ML Architecture

📥

Feature engineering — geospatial + temporal features from public APIs

🧠

XGBoost ensemble — per-hazard sub-models combined into composite score

🔍

SHAP explainability — shows which hazards drive the score

💲

Financial calibration — score mapped to expected annual loss ($)

Stage 2

RAG-Powered
Sustainability Strategist

An AI agent that takes the risk scores and — through RAG over sustainability knowledge — generates a customized action plan.

Agent Tools

🔍

RAG Retriever — queries sustainability knowledge base (LEED, LL97, IRA credits)

📊

Portfolio Analyzer — parses client energy data, certifications, building profiles

💲

ROI Calculator — estimates retrofit costs, savings, and payback period

🌐

Incentive Finder — matches property location to available rebates & tax credits

📑

Report Generator — creates client-ready strategy deck with visualizations

Knowledge Base (RAG)

CERTIFICATIONS

LEED v4.1, WELL v2, ENERGY STAR certification guides

REGULATIONS

LL97 (NYC), BERDO (Boston), EU Taxonomy, CSRD compliance

INCENTIVES

IRA tax credits, state rebates, utility programs from DSIRE

TECHNOLOGY

Heat pumps, LED, solar, BMS upgrades — specs, costs & ROI

🧠 Orchestrated by LangGraph — a state-machine agent framework for multi-step reasoning with tool use.

Demo Scenario

A JLL broker walks into
a client meeting

The client is a real estate fund with 50 properties across the US Southeast. They want to understand their climate exposure.

1

Upload portfolio

Broker uploads a CSV of 50 property addresses. ClimateNexus geocodes each one.

2

Instant risk scoring

In 30 seconds, every property is scored. 12 flagged high flood risk, 8 flagged extreme heat.

3

AI generates strategy

The agent auto-creates tailored recommendations — flood barriers for the 12, cool-roof retrofits for the 8, net-zero roadmap for all 50.

4

Client-ready output

A branded PDF deck with risk heatmap, action plan, $2.3M in incentives identified, and 34% projected energy savings.
