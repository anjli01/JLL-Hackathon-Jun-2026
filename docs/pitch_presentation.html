03 — Risks & How We Solve Them

Every hazard. Every
property. Every market.

ClimateNexus doesn't just flag risk — it bridges the gap between a risk signal and a prescriptive, financially-calibrated action. Here's how we tackle each hazard dimension.

🌊

Flood Risk

HIGH IMPACT
❌ The Risk

FEMA Zone AE/VE designation, SFHA status, low elevation — leading to forced insurance renegotiation and stranded assets in coastal portfolios.

✅ ClimateNexus Fix

Real-time FEMA API scoring → recommends flood barriers, elevated mechanicals, FEMA Hazard Mitigation Grants, and resilience-first underwriting.

🌡️

Extreme Heat Stress

HIGH IMPACT
❌ The Risk

80+ projected extreme heat days, high cooling degree days (Phoenix, Dallas, Austin) → HVAC overload, tenant comfort crises, energy cost spikes.

✅ ClimateNexus Fix

NOAA 30-year climate normals → cool-roof retrofits, heat pump upgrades, IRA §48 energy efficiency tax credits, shade & greenery interventions.

🔥

Wildfire Proximity

MEDIUM IMPACT
❌ The Risk

WUI-adjacent properties in California and Colorado face annual burn probability risk, insurance non-renewal, and devaluation of surrounding assets.

✅ ClimateNexus Fix

USFS burn probability scoring → ember-resistant materials, defensible space planning, wildfire insurance optimization, and risk-informed capital planning.

⚡

Seismic & Transition Risk

HIGH IMPACT
❌ The Risk

Seismic Design Category D/E (SF, LA, Seattle) + regulatory exposure from LL97, BERDO, Title 24 — creating compliance fines and retrofit liability.

✅ ClimateNexus Fix

USGS Design Maps API + built-in regulation engine → compliance pathway acceleration, LEED/WELL certification roadmaps, and retrofit prioritization ranked by ROI.

07 — Tech Stack

Production-grade.
Open-source. Scalable.

API Framework
FastAPI + Uvicorn
Async REST API with Pydantic validation, Swagger docs, and batch endpoints supporting up to 200 properties.
ML Scoring Engine
XGBoost Ensemble + SHAP
6-hazard weighted composite scorer with SHAP-style decomposition for full explainability of every risk score.
AI Agent Framework
LangGraph State Machine
Multi-step agentic workflow: analyze → retrieve → find incentives → calculate ROI → generate strategy.
Language Model
Google Gemini 2.5 Flash
Powers the strategy generation, chat refinement, and narrative synthesis across all recommendations.
RAG Knowledge Base
ChromaDB + Gemini Embeddings
14 curated sustainability docs (LEED, IRA, LL97, BERDO) chunked to 800 tokens and semantically retrieved.
Frontend
Streamlit + Folium + Plotly
Multi-page app with interactive risk heatmap, SHAP waterfall charts, chat interface, and PDF export.
